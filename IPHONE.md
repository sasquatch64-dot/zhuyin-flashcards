## App URL

https://sasquatch64-dot.github.io/zhuyin-flashcards/

(Hosted from the public `zhuyin-flashcards` repo — the main trainer repo is private and cannot use GitHub Pages on a free plan.)

## Install on iPhone 14 Pro Max

1. Open the URL in **Safari** (not Chrome, not Cursor)
2. Tap the **Share** button
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Name it **Zhuyin** → **Add**
5. Open it from your Home Screen — it runs like an app

## Sound on iPhone

- Tap **Speak pronunciation** after flipping a card
- iOS Safari blocks speech until you tap a button
- Use headphones if you want clearer tone practice

## Offline

After the first Safari visit, the app caches itself. The Home Screen icon still works without Wi‑Fi for the flashcards (speech uses the phone’s built-in Chinese voices).
